The purpose of this site is to introduce players already familiar with the table-top role-playing market to a family style rpg in the realm of Hylandia based on a 2d10 die system that aims to use game mechanics and planning structures that support a narrative-first high fantasy role-playing game.

Target Audience

The target audience is experienced role-players interested in a family-oriented game for gentle learning and fun.

Scenario 2: What are some cool monsters?

This section will provide information on a few shadowfeel beasties that could be encountered. It will be a gallery of images that each has an overlay with descriptive text including a name, home, instincts, and miscellaneous details.

The dreadlegs is a massive spider with ten eyes and ten legs from the shadowfeel realm - it feeds off of anxiety, fear, and unsurety. While it can be weakened by working to calm those nearby or involved, once one of these spawn it needs to be faced head on - paralytic bite and all. When a dreadlegs manifests it prefers to find dark corners that have enough height to keep it off the ground. Rather than attack head on, dreadlegs usually sit, watch, and wait continuing to feed on growing fear until it decides it is time to move on.

Color Schema

The color scheme will be based off purple and green with green being the primary background color, and purple and it's opposite (a golden yellow) will be used for calls to action.

Typography

The heading will be one appropriate for a "natural fantasy" style rpg game without being too flowery. The base text will be appealing and unique, with a backup of serif.
